ScoreCard

Beta paper grading assistant for real classroom workflows.

Beta
Teacher-controlled beta

Grade paper work from photos.

ScoreCard helps teachers move faster without giving up control. Beta supports multiple choice only for now — no number responses or short answers yet. Add an answer key, upload or take photos of student work, review every suggested score, then save only what you approve.

Step 01Set up the answer keyChoose multiple choice, enter how many questions, pick answer choices, point value, and the correct answers.
Step 02Take clear photosUse bright light, keep the whole page in frame, flatten the paper, and take one photo per side.
Step 03Review carefullyCheck handwriting, skipped questions, low-confidence answers, blurry pages, and anything cropped.
Step 04Save as draft gradesExport results or add them to Simple Grades only after the teacher approves each score.

New ScoreCard run

This is the front-end beta flow. ScoreCard currently grades multiple choice only — no number responses or short answers yet.

Drop student photos here
JPG/PNG · one page per image · multiple choice sheets only
Beta limit: ScoreCard can grade multiple choice only right now. Number answers, free response, essays, and short answers are not supported yet.

Be careful before scanning

ScoreCard should help teachers, not replace teacher judgment.

  • Retake blurry, dark, tilted, or cropped photos.
  • Make sure the whole answer area and student name are visible.
  • Use multiple choice sheets only; number and short-answer grading is not ready yet.
  • Review every low-confidence answer before saving.
  • Do not upload private student information you do not need.
  • Final grades should stay teacher-approved, not auto-posted.
Beta promise: ScoreCard creates draft suggestions only. The teacher reviews, edits, and approves before anything goes into the gradebook.
